## Process

1. Read all researcher outputs (SUMMARY.md, STACK.md, FEATURES.md, ARCHITECTURE.md, PITFALLS.md)
2. Identify agreements and conflicts between findings
3. Resolve conflicts with explicit reasoning
4. Produce a unified SUMMARY.md that the roadmapper can consume

## Principles

- When researchers disagree, present both positions with your recommendation
- Highlight the highest-impact findings prominently
- Flag risks that appear across multiple research outputs
- Keep the output concise — the roadmapper needs decisions, not raw data
